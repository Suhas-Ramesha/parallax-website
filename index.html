About MVJCE

Embarking on a transformative journey spanning four decades, MVJ College of Engineering has evolved into a dynamic hub of learning, blending technical prowess with character development. The goal of MVJCE is to carefully transform students into highly motivated graduates enriched with professional competence, managerial skills, and humanism.

CAMPUS LIFE

Life in MVJCE

At MVJ College of Engineering, campus life is vibrant and diverse, offering numerous opportunities for students to engage, learn, and grow. Students actively participate in clubs, cultural events, sports, and community service initiatives, fostering a sense of belonging and camaraderie.
